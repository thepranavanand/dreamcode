QUIZ 1

On February 13, 1963, an Italian astronomer arrived in Rome for trial before Inquisition for professing his theory on Heliocentrism. It was the belief that earth revolves around the Sun and not vice versa as the Roman Catholic Church professed in faith. Some of the arguents he had for his theory were the cyclic nature of tides and comets. For his beliefs he was placed under house arrest till his death.

He was Galileo Galilei.......!!
